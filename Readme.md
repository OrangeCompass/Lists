Vector, Your Path to Clarity.

- Technical Stack (For devs, internal)
Vector: A list making solution that utilizes fastapi as backend for speed, json file (eventually mongodb for flexible scalibility) as storage and Figma for intuitive UI.

- The Elevator Pitch/ Core Value Proposition/ One line summary (For App Store, external)
Vector: The Unified List Hub: Simplify and organize every kind of list on the go, effortlessly. Offload your mind. Find your way through mental clutter.

- User Benefit/ Pain Point Solutions (Marketing Copy)
Your mind is for ideas, not inventory. It’s time to offload, organize, and get back your focus.
Vector is the single hub designed to help you find your way through mental clutter. We go beyond simple To-Do lists—we help you capture all of life's lists: from urgent lecture notes and spontaneous business ideas, to grocery runs and detailed growth strategies.
Stop trying to remember everything. Let Vector take care of what you need to offload from your mind.
